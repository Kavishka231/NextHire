FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Can guests search jobs?

Yes. Guests can browse and inspect jobs, but applying through NextHire requires registration.

Can companies post immediately?

Company accounts must be approved and verified by an admin before posting jobs.

Can candidates apply with different details?

Yes. Candidates can use profile autofill or enter role-specific application details.
